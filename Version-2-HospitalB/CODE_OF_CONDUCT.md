# Medi OS Community Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and inclusive environment for all contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful, patient, and supportive.
- Provide constructive feedback, welcome new ideas, and credit collaborators.
- Follow all project guidelines, especially regarding privacy and security of healthcare data.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Sharing protected health information or confidential credentials.
- Knowingly introducing insecure or deceptive code paths.

## Reporting
If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code, please contact the maintainers at `conduct@medios.health`. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the project community.

By contributing to Medi OS Kiroween Edition v2.0, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
